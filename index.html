When Chaos came,

He gave us two blessings. One of which is your heart. A Witch's Heart. How will you respond to His kindness?

Will you carry His will and slay Demons roaming this world, or shall you rebel and abadon your destined path?

Will you carry the burden of Witch's Heart and face lurking threats and solve thousand years old problem in this world?

About Game

Top Down Boss Rush

Tale of Witch's Heart is fast peaced action combat game. You will be facing lot of different Enemies and Bosses in huge semi-open hand drawn pixelart world filled with secrets, NPCs and enemies to slain!

Fight however you like

In this game you can choose between different weapons and skills to create build that suits you best. Here skill and knowledge of your weapon is everything that matters!

Uncover secrets

You will meet many NPC with their own goals. Use them to find about world and story of this land or your own destiny. You can decide to work with them or not. They will help you or try to use you for their own purposes. Its up to you whom to trust.

Freedom of actions

You can go almost everywhere you want, you can go straight to final boss if you wish so. You can complete NPCs quest or stab them in the back. You are the only one person that can change and save or destroy this world.
